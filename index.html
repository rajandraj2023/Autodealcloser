What is AutoDealCloser?

AutoDealCloser is an AI based technology for Car Dealers to sell one car or all cars in their lots using "QR Codes" at any time & with our "Virtual Sales Assistant", they can also sell more cars online in variable price range. We also have our "Marketplace" where you can sell/buy your cars using our technologies.

Inquiries for demo, partnerships & investment are welcome. BTW, we have needs for some part time executives as co-founders too. Ask us about our beta gift (Price Comparison Portal Showing Your Competition).

Who we are NOT?

We do not work on getting you more dollars for your cars OR we do not help in your repossession problem OR we do not bring additional traffic to your website or your dealerships. Our Marketplace brings additional buyers for you though. If you advertise correctly, additional tech savvy buyers OR do-it-yourself buyers OR buyers who are avoiding dealerships will show up. We engage your buyers. We remove their concerns. With AutoDealCloser, you can sell more without adding more salespersons.
