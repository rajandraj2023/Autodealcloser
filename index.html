What is auto deal closer?

AutoDealCloser is an AI based technology company offering its own marketplace and technologies for online sellers for their websites and stores. We provide three products:

  • Virtual Sales Assistant – API based – You can use this in place of a button, or our AI/NLP based chatbot too.
  • QR Codes for your cars & store
  • Marketplace where we market your cars and show all technologies coming together.

Inquiries for demo, partnerships & investment are welcome. We also have needs for some “part time executives/co-founders”.
